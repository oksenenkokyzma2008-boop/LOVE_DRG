So what about main loop of this game

The core loop of Deep Rock Galactic is: pick a mission, drop into procedurally generated caves, mine resources, deposite them in the MULE, complete objectives, fight off alien swarms, and escape via the Drop Pod. Back at the Space Rig, you upgrade gear, unlock cosmetics, and restock before heading back down again. Rock and Stone!

Now let's talk about short kings of this game. Four main classes

Every class is fun to play with, but what specialties do they have ?

Class Short Role Main Pros Main Cons
Driller Terrain control, digging shortcuts Fast map shaping, crowd control, great mobility for team Weak at long-range fights, ammo can run out fast
Engineer Builds defenses and support platforms Strong area control, boosts team movement, great DPS setup Needs setup time, less effective alone
Gunner Heavy frontline firepower Highest sustained damage, shields save team, strong in swarms Slow movement, weak mobility, limited utility
Scout Recon, lighting, mobility Fastest class, great for objectives, reveals caves with light Low survivability, weaker in large fights alone

Main enemyes of DRG(exept gravitation)

The caves of Hoxxes IV are home to countless hostile creatures. While most enemies are Glyphids, you'll also encounter flying threats, robotic enemies, and massive bosses. Every mission features random enemy spawns, keeping each expedition unpredictable.

Fight over 50 unique hostile enemies, ranging from swarming Glyphids and flying Mactera to deadly cave predators, Rival Tech robots , and massive bosses.

In Deep Rock Galactic, each class has its own Primary and Secondary weapons, throwables, and tools, all upgradeable with powerful Overclocks that change how they work—ranging from flamethrowers and miniguns to smart rifles and plasma guns.
